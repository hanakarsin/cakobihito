Elena Moussa - Age, Family, Bio

Best recognized as the wife of Fox News' Greg Gutfeld, host of Red Eye with Greg Gutfeld.

Before Fame

In June 2011, she joined Twitter and posted the inaugural tweet reading "I'm a huge fan of @greggutfeld!"

Trivia

She owns the clothing rental company the Moussa Project.

Family Life

Born and raised in Russia, she later moved to London with her family. That was where she met Gutfeld.

Associated With

Her husband has worked alongside a number of other journalists including Bill Schulz.
