Mar 7, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

WOODZ - Age, Family, Bio

Became known as a member of the boyband Uniq with hits such as "EOEO," "My Dream," and "Listen to Me." He later formed the musical duo MOLA (Make Our Life Awesome) with rapper Nathan, known for their single "My Way." He's also enjoyed success as a solo artist under the moniker Luizy with his debut hit "Recipe."

Before Fame

He previously was a member of the Brazilian soccer team the Corinthians before joining Uniq in 2014.

Trivia

He and Uniq were named KU Music Asian Music Awards 'Best New Artist' in April 2015. He released his first full-length album in March of 2021, titled Set. 

Family Life

He was born Cho Seungyoun in Seoul, South Korea, and later changed to the stage name Luizy as a solo artist. He has also lived in the Philippines, Brazil and the US.

Associated With

He and rapper Flowsik of Aziatix had a 1 on 1 battle on the reality competition show Show Me the Money before teaming up together on the single "Recipe."
