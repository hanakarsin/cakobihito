Christopher Martin (Reggae Singer) - Age, Family, Bio

Reggae or dancehall singer and songwriter who has released popular singles such as "Take My Wings," "Giving It," and "I'll Be Your Driver."In 2013, he signed a recording contract with the New York based reggae label VP Records.

Before Fame

He won the 2005 Digicel Rising Stars talent show competition.

Trivia

In 2016, he had the opportunity to tour throughout Zimbabwe.

Family Life

He was born to Cleveland and Maxine Martin.

Associated With

He has shared the stage with a number of other artists including pop singer Michael Bolton.
