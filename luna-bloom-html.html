Luna Bloom - Age, Family, Bio

Short-form autonomous sensory meridian response content creator who rose to fame by sharing trigger sounds on her lunabloomasmr TikTok account. Her videos often focus on combining makeup and ASMR. She has more than 1.7 million followers on the platform.

Before Fame

Her first TikTok ASMR video was captioned "Getting the bad energy out, and sprinkling in some good energy."

Trivia

Outside of TikTok, she also creates longer-form content on YouTube. She posts modeling selfies and cosplay photos on Instagram.

Family Life

She was born in Colombia. Her wife Ronnie Bloom is a life coach.

Associated With

She's said that she would love to collab with actress Aubrey Plaza.
