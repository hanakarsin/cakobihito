Nigerians React As DJ Cuppy's Professor Tags Her A Scholar On Matriculation Day

A professor at Oxford University named Jonathan Shalit OBE has stirred reactions after tagging billionaire daughter, DJ Cuppy a scholar.

Naija News understands that billionaire daughter, Ifeoluwa Florence Otedola, better known as DJ Cuppy was accepted into Oxford University, England, for a master’s degree in March, 2021.

The billionaire heiress had her matriculation day at the University of Oxford, England, today, 18th October, and shared beautiful photos via her Instagram handle.

Amongst people who celebrated her is one of the DJ’s professors identified as Jonathan Shalit OBE who tagged her a scholar.

Sharing her photo, he wrote; ‘A great scholar @Cuppymusic’

However, fans of the DJ stormed the comment section of the tweet, leaving embarrassing remarks.
