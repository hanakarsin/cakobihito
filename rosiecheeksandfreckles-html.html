Kelsey Simone - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube beauty enthusiast with a self-titled channel which was previously called RosieCheeksAndFreckles. She has gained renown for her frequent beauty and makeup tutorials, as well as fashion advice vlogs. She has been known for her sleek aesthetic and her black and white themed Instagram page. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel on April 11, 2013. She posted her first video there on May 15, 2014.

Trivia

She has garnered more than 1.6 million subscribers to her YouTube channel. One of her most popular videos there, "Everyday makeup tutorial! | updated 2016," has earned more than 850,000 total views. She has also collaborated with the jewelry company Argento Vivo. 

Family Life

She featured her mother Liz in a January 24, 2016 video titled "MEET MY MOM."

Associated With

Her reaction video to Justin Bieber's Purpose album release was her first viral video.
