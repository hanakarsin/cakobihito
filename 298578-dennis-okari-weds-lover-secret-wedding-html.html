Dennis Okari weds new lover in secret wedding

NTV news anchor Dennis Okari is official off the market.

TUKO.co.ke learnt the husky-voiced TV personality exchanged marital vows with his new lover Naomi Joy on the morning of Friday, February 15, at the Ridgeways Baptist Church in Kiambu.

Okari and Naomi's wedding saw the attendance of the who is who in the entertainment industry including his colleagues from NTV and other media houses.

According to sources intimate to Okari, the TV personality wanted his wedding under wraps and went for an invite-only affair, with some of his guests said to have been unaware of the venue until Friday, February 15, morning.

TUKO.co.ke learnt the wedding reception was later held at the Royal Gardens Grounds on Kiambu Road.

The wedding came just weeks after the news anchor went down on one knee and popped the question before Naomi at the Eka Hotel.

It should be noted Okari married his new wife about two years after parting ways with ex-wife Betty Kyallo.

The two made headlines with their colourful garden wedding but parted ways only six months later, citing irreconcilable differences.
