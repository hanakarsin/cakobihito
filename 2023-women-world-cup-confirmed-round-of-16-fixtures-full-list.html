Confirmed Round Of 16 Fixtures (Full List)

All countries that made it to the round of 16 stages in the ongoing 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup in Australia and New Zealand have been confirmed.

This follows the conclusion of matches in the group stage of the competition on Thursday.

In Thursday’s games, Morocco defeated Colombia 1-0, while South Korea drew 1-1 with Germany.

With the development, Colombia and Morocco join other countries that had earlier booked their slots in the round of 16 stage.

They are Nigeria, England, Denmark, Jamaica, South Africa, Japan, Spain, Sweden, United States, France, Australia, Netherlands, Norway and Switzerland.

The Round of 16 fixtures will commence on Saturday and would be played through Tuesday.

Below are the full Round of 16 fixtures and dates:

Switzerland vs Spain – Saturday

Japan vs Norway – Saturday,

Netherlands vs South Africa – Sunday

Sweden vs United States – Sunday

England vs Nigeria – Monday

Australia vs Denmark – Monday

Colombia vs Jamaica – Tuesday

France vs Morocco – Tuesday
