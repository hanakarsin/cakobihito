Photos From Kim Kardashian &amp; Kanye West Wedding In Italy

The first official photos from the Italian wedding of Kim Kardashian and Kanye West have been finally released. A renowned Internet portal E! Online shared some exclusive photos of the newlyweds on May 27, 2014, Tuesday.

The lovebirds were shining on their wedding day at the Forte di Belvedere. They said “I do” in front of their closest relatives and friends, as the ceremony itself was closed for outsiders.

* The couple are sharing a newlywed kiss. The impressive wall made of white flowers was put up right after the bride and the groom finished telling their vows. 

* Just-married couple is walking down the aisle. The face of their cute daughter North West can be spotted in the picture. The baby girl watches her smiling parents, sits safely in the hands of the grandmother, Kris Jenner.

* The exclusive photos show Kim's wedding dress in full.

The ceremony was served by a good friend of the newlyweds Pastor Rich Wilkerson Jr. from Miami. 

The wedding party has reportedly included a four-course meal and 7-tiered wedding cake for the guests. 

* Another kiss by the newlyweds

Kanye West prepared an impressive 20-minute-long speech for Kim. Other speakers, who toasted the newlyweds included the parents, Kris and Bruce Jenner, the relatives and the friends. A serenade by famous American R&B, hip hop and neo soul artiste, John Legend, was especially touching and emotional.

The couple had their first dance as the husband and the wife during his song "All of Me".

* Celebrity couple in leather outfits

It would be recalled that the controversial TV reality show star and a mother of one Kim K and the renowned hip hop artiste, songwriter and producer Kanye said their vows in Florence, Italy, on May 24, 2014, Sunday.

Again, our best congratulations to the happy couple!
