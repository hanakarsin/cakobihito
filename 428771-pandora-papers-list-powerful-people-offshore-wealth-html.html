Pandora Papers: List of Heads of State, Politicians and Celebrities Who Have Offshore Wealth

  • The Pandora Papers compiled by over 600 investigative journalists analysed 12 million files from popular tax havens
  • World leaders including Kenya's president Uhuru Kenyatta and members of his family were named as having accumulated offshore wealth
  • Singer Shakira, married to Barcelona defender Gerard Pique, was found guilty in Spain for evading paying KSh 1.8 billion in taxes from 2012 to 2014

The Pandora Papers, published on Sunday, October 3, by the International Consortium of Investigative Journalists (ICIJ) exposed powerful individuals across the world hiding their wealth in offshore accounts.

The over 600 journalists from 150 media companies spread across 117 countries analysed 12 million files leaked from popular tax havens such as Panama, the British Virgin Islands, Bermuda, The Cayman Islands and Hong Kong.

The expose' named heads of states, politicians, celebrities and sports stars.

1. King Abdullah II - Jordan

The Jordanian King was reported to have accumulated KSh 11.05 billion through secret companies based in the United Kingdom (UK) and the United States (US).

2. Andrej Rabis

Czech Prime Minister Andrej Babis bought an estate in French Riviera using proceeds from offshore companies. He allegedly transferred KSh 2.4 billion secretly.

3. Ilham Aliyev -Azerbaijan

The Azerbaijan president and his family were involved in deals worth KSh 60 billion.

The family used the billions to buy 17 properties in Britain.

4. Tony Blair - United Kingdom

Former UK prime minister Tony Blair who works as a consultant for President Uhuru Kenyatta was reported to have saved KSh 44 million in taxes by buying property in the British Virgin Islands.

5. Svetlana Krivonogikh - Russia

The Russian woman acquired a Monaco apartment with the help of Russian president Vladimir Putin.

She used an offshore company incorporated on the Caribbean island of Tortola.

6. Volodymyr Zelensky - Ukraine

Zelensky who took the oath of office on May 20, 2019, purchased three properties in London using a network of offshore companies despite his anti-corruption stance.

7. Sebastian Pinera - Chile

The Chilean president whose family owns the Dominga mining project was involved in shady deals with offshore companies in BVI.

He denied the allegations.

8. Shakira

According to ICIJ, Shakira and other celebrities, including Ringo Starr, Claudia Schiffer, Julio Iglesias, have set up offshore companies in tax havens.

The Colombina-born singer, married to Barcelona defender Gerard Pique, was found guilty in Spain for evading paying KSh 1.8 billion in taxes from 2012 to 2014.

She used offshore companies that managed the production of her music to evade the taxes.

9. Pakistan ministers, generals

Over 700 Pakistanis were named in the leak, dubbed the biggest in history.

Water Minister Moonis Elahi and his Finance counterpart Shaukat Tarin topped the list in the Middle East country.

10. Ex-Hong Kong leaders

Leung Chun-Ying who led Hong Kong from 2012 to 2017 sold shares in a Japanese company but did not declare income from it.

Tung Chee-Hwa, the city's first executive set up offshore companies after he retired in 2005.

Kenyatta family

The Kenyatta family featured prominently in the Pandora Papers leak.

Six members of the first family including President Uhuru Kenyatta and Mama Ngina Kenyatta were said to have accumulated offshore wealth secretly.
