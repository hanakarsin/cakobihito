April 23 Horoscope and Zodiac

Your sign's ruling planet is Venus and as you were born in the first Decan, or part, of the Taurus sign, you receive a double helping of Venus's power. Venus is the planet of harmony and subsequently, directly links to your sociability, values, cooperation and appreciation of beauty. More so than any of the Taurus Decans, your planetary influence makes you loving and materialistic. You enjoy to be loved, as well as showing love, which explains why you are such a "touchy feely" person. Ever faithful, you value security in relationships and are quite loyal. You require aesthetically pleasing surroundings and probably are known for your good taste. In love, find a partner that shares in your loyalty and appreciation of the finer things in life, as this will bring you the most happiness.
