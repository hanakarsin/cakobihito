Breaking: Wizkid wins Grammy award with Brown Skin Girl song alongside Beyonce

- Much loved Nigerian musician, Wizkid, has won his very first Grammy award with his feature in Brown Skin Girl

- Among those who vied for the best music video are Harry Styles' Adore You, Woodkid's Goliath and Future's Life is Good to mention some

- Fans of Wizkid has taken to social media to celebrate star boy's massive win

Wizkid has won in the category of Best Music Video alongside Beyonce for Brown Skin Girl. The reception of the award has been massive on social media with fans of both artiste reacting joyously.

It should be noted that this year's edition of the award of the ceremony was earlier scheduled for January but had to be shifted because of the coronavirus pandemic restrictions.

Also in the best music video category were:

  • Adore You by Harry Styles
  • Life Is Good by Future featuring Drake
  • Lockdown by Anderson Paak
  • Goliath by Woodkid

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that the Smile crooner revealed some details about himself in an interview as he spoke on love, family, and how he is able to ignore social media hate among other things.

Also, Wizkid spoke about his Made in Lagos album and how it seemed to have family and love as its major theme.

Wizkid said that his family is everything to him and that it is what keeps him grounded. The singer added that his three sons are his purpose in life and it is important for him to include that in his music.
