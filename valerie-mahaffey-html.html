Valerie Mahaffey - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who won an Emmy Award for Northern Exposure and also appeared on Desperate Housewives and Wings.

Before Fame

She was born in Indonesia and moved to Texas when she was sixteen years old. She went on to receive her BFA from the University of Texas at Austin.

Trivia

She debuted on Broadway in the musical Rex.

Family Life

She married and had a child with Joseph Kell.

Associated With

She and Cynthia Geary both appeared on Northern Exposure.
