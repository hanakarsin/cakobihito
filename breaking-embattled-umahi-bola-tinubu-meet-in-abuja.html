Embattled Umahi, Bola Tinubu Meet In Abuja

The embattled Governor of Ebonyi State, David Umahi on Friday met with the National Leader of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Naija News reports that the chieftains of the ruling party met in Abuja.

This was made known by the Special Assistant to the Governor of Ebonyi State, Francis Nwaze.

He however failed to reveal details of the meeting while sharing photos from the meeting in a post on Facebook.

“Presidential hopeful and Governor of Ebonyi State, Engr. David Umahi, FNSE, FNATE meets Alh. Bola Ahmed Tinubu in Abuja on Friday, March 11, 2022,” he wrote.

Tinubu and Umahi are among the APC members who have declared intention in succeeding President Muhammadu Buhari in 2023.

Umahi is also battling to retain his position as governor after an Abuja High Court had ordered his sack and replacement.
