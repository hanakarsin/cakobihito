Hazel Moder - Age, Family, Bio

Best recognized as the daughter of Pretty Woman star Julia Roberts and cinematographer Daniel Moder. 

Before Fame

Her parents met on the set of 2000's The Mexican and two years later would get married. 

Trivia

She appeared opposite her mother in a small role in 2016's Mother's Day. 

Family Life

She has a twin brother, Phinnaeus "Finn" Walter Moder, and a younger brother, Henry Daniel Moder. 

Associated With

Her cousin is American Horror Story star Emma Roberts.
