Portable Fumes After Being Sprayed Fake Naira Notes In Port Harcourt Club

Controversial Nigerian singer, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable, has rained curses on party lovers who sprayed fake naira notes on him in a club. 

Naija News reports that the singer was recently in Rivers State for an event, and had retired to the club for some fun over the weekend.

Hours after the celebration, Portable via his Instastory, gave an update on the money he earned following the successful show.

However, it appeared some of the funds sprayed on him during the event were fake.

Reacting to the development, the Zazuu crooner who lamented bitterly following the development rained curses on party lovers in the state for such deceit.

Portable call out came hours after he stated that he does not have a plan B if the music does not work for him.

The singer argued that he can fight his battle himself without any external help from colleagues and allies in the industry.

Portable advised his fans to learn to stand on their own because nobody else can change their situation but themselves.
