Emmy Clarke - Age, Family, Bio

Movie and Television actress who gained fame for her appearances in My House in Umbria and 2006's Fur.

Before Fame

Trivia

In 2004, she won a Young Artist Award for her performance in My House in Umbria.

Family Life

She was born in Mineola, New York and moved to Houston, Texas with her parents. Her birth name is Mary Elizabeth and she created her name Emmy from her initials.

Associated With

She appeared alongside actress Maggie Smith in the film My House in Umbria.
