Billionaire Philip Ng Chee Tat says life and materialism without Jesus is empty

Philip Ng Chee Tat, the richest man in Singapore estimated to be worth $8.7 billion has said that life and materialism without Jesus is a broken life.

Singapore’s richest man, Philip Ng Chee Tat, revealed that he’s an ardent Christian while stating that life and materialism without Jesus Christ is empty when he was asked about key success to life.

Mr Chee Tat indicated that “all of us are broken.”

He emphasized that, “I have discovered that all of us are broken, we all have a missing piece. For me, I discovered that the missing piece was God in Jesus Christ.”

Singapore’s largest developer said he was always in search of ‘‘a better life, a better purpose, better me [and] a better everything. I was looking at all the wrong things.”

He disclosed that when he realized there was ‘‘no better me, or better things without Jesus, then it all snapped in place.”
