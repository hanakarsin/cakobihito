Burna Boy, AKA Attacks Club Patron

After jointly winning the Best African Collabo at the All African Music Awards (AFRIMA), South Africa rapper AKA and his Nigerian counterpart Burna Boy headed to the nightclub to celebrate their winning.

But before they could start celebrating, the two singers and their cohorts caused a stir. The singers hit the car of a night club patron and also inflicted injuries on the man.

A night club patron who was returning from a bar early in the morning of Monday, November 16 has accused musicians Burna Boy and AKA of molesting him and running him over with a vehicle.

According to the victim:

"This morning around the 4:30am, I was heading back from G12 Bar, Oniru with my friend that was celebrating his bday when BURNABOY and HIS CREW which included AKA and some other unknown artistes PURPOSELY HIT MY CAR with His COUPE LEXUS CONVERTIBLE CAR in front of CHEVY VIEW ESTATE, CHEVRON, LEKKI.
