Barry Van Dyke - Age, Family, Bio

Best known for his role as Lieutenant Detective Steve Sloan on the television series, Diagnosis: Murder and also appeared on shows like The Love Boat and Remington Steele.

Before Fame

His first role was as the 9-year-old violinist, Florian on The Dick Van Dyke Show.

Trivia

He is an avid motorcycle enthusiast who has participated in protest rides to preserve the rights of motorcyclists.

Family Life

He has 4 children with Mary Carey.

Associated With

He and his world-famous father, Dick Van Dyke, worked together on shows like The Dick Van Dyke Show and Diagnosis: Murder.
