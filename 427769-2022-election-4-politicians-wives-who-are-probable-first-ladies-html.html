The country is headed to the polls next year and all the political players are currently strategising to see that they clinch the coveted top seat.

This will be the fifth slot for the presidency and it is rush against time for the contenders bearing in mind what awaits the winner who will always enjoy the trappings of power and all the privileges that come with the top office.

Known to many, the assumption of the president-elect is also sweet news to their wives/husbands. This is because they automatically assume the First lady/Man and are accorded the much respect it deserves.

As the clock ticks, a number of male aspirants are holding on hope that they will be able to ascend to power so that their wives can become the country’s, first lady.

The question that most citizens might be asking themselves is who could be the next first lady when the 2022 polls are held.

First on the list is Ida Odinga, the wife of former prime minister Raila Odinga. She has witnessed political transitions over the years and like many other women, she is also hopeful that she might be the next country’s, first lady.

Raila’s wife is also a graduate of arts from the University of Nairobi and has been widely seen as a strong pillar of her husband.

Her passion has always been to inspire and protect the girl child. Early this month, she publicly took a jibe at defilers while calling on all county commissioners to crack their whip and bring justice to the girl child.

“Girls should concentrate on their studies and wait. When you ruin a girl, you ruin a nation. I am looking for a day when the defilers will be taken to court, I will personally be there to see how they are charged,” she said.

Second, on the list is Rachel Ruto. She is the wife to deputy president William Ruto and has earned the title, the second lady of Kenya.

She holds a master of arts from the Catholic University of Eastern Africa. As the second lady, she has been an advocate for women's empowerment. This has earned her international awards.

Pauline Musyoka is another possible entrant in occupying the house on the hill. She has served as the second lady when his husband Kalonzo Musyoka was the vice president.

Musyoka’s wife has kept a low profile and away from the glaring public eye. His husband has already declared that his name has to be on the ballot in next year’s polls.

The Wiper flag bearer has also been crisscrossing the country while popularising his political bid through the wiper ticket.
