Danny Ramirez - Age, Family, Bio

Colombian/Mexican-American actor who broke through in 2018 for his roles in the TV series On My Block as Mario Martinez and the film Assassination Nation as Diamond. He was also cast as Chip in Valley Girl. He gained fame in 2022 for playing Lt. Mickey 'Fanboy' Garcia in the movie Top Gun: Maverick.

Before Fame

While attending New York University's Tisch School of the Arts, he made his TV debut in The Affair. He also booked roles in NBC's Blindspot and a feature called Rapid Eye Movement while in school.

Trivia

He was a guest-star in an episode of Orange Is the New Black and had a three-episode arc in The Gifted. He has amassed over 20 acting credits throughout his ongoing career. 

Family Life

He was born in Chicago, Illinois. He has a sister.

Associated With

He and Bella Thorne were both cast in Assassination Nation.
