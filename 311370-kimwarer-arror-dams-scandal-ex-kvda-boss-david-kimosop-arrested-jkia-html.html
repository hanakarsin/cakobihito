Kimwarer, Arror dams scandal: Ex-KVDA boss David Kimosop arrested at JKIA

Former Kerio Valley Development Authority boss David Kimosop has been arrested by detectives from Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI).

Kimosop was nabbed at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) on Friday, July 26, morning after jetting back from Tanzania.

His arrest was confirmed by DCI detectives who noted the suspect was still being held by police as he awaited to be arraigned.

The ex managing director is facing charges of abuse of office, flouting procurement laws, facilitating irregular payments among other charges that led to loss of billions of tax payers money.

He is accused alongside 27 others including former Treasury Cabinet Secretary Henry Rotich and his Principal Secretary Kamau Thugge who were charged on Tuesday, July 23.

Others charged over the same include Tourism and Wildlife Principal Secretary Susan Koech and National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) boss Geoffrey Wahungu.

Kimosop was the only high profile officer who was yet to be arrested in connection with the scam.

His lawyer Katwa Kigen had on Tuesday requested the court to grant him more time to present his client for plea-taking since he had travelled outside the country.
