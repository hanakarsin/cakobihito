Demi Lovato - Age, Family, Bio

Actor and singer who appeared in Camp Rock with The Jonas Brothers and whose album Don't Forget was certified Gold by the RIAA. They also played Sonny Munroe in the Disney Channel's series Sonny with a Chance from 2009 to 2011. 

Before Fame

They began playing piano at the age of seven. 

Trivia

They starred in the 2009 film Princess Protection Program and has called John Mayer their biggest musical influence. They were a judge on the second and third seasons of The X Factor (US).

Family Life

Their parents are Patrick Lovato and Dianna De La Garza. They have an older sister named Dallas, a younger half-sister, actress Madison De La Garza, and an older half-sister, Amber. In late 2019, they dated model Austin Wilson. They dated Wilmer Valderrama from 2010 to 2016. They also dated Joe Jonas and Luke Rockhold. In July 2020, they got engaged to Max Ehrich, but the couple split up two months later. In May 2021, they announced that they identified as nonbinary. 

Associated With

They earned great reviews through their performance in Camp Rock with Nick Jonas.
