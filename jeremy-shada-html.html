Jeremy Shada - Age, Family, Bio

Actor turned musician who became a member of the Make Out Monday in 2014. He voiced the pure-hearted hero Finn on Cartoon Network's Adventure Time as well as Lance on Voltron: Legendary Defender. He also starred as Reggie on the Netflix series Julie and the Phantoms.

Before Fame

He had his first acting role at age 7 in the 2004 TV movie Miracle Run. 

Trivia

He made guest appearances on television shows like ER, Ghost Whisperer, and Parenthood.

Family Life

He has two older brothers, Josh and Zack. He married Carolynn Rowland in 2020.

Associated With

He played the younger version of Dominic Monaghan's character, Charlie Pace, on the television show Lost.
