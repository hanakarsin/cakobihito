Mizzy - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star, whose real name is Bacari-Bronze Constantine O'Garro, gained fame for his videos in which he committed criminal activities, which he refers to as pranks, in the name of gaining popularity online. His videos involved trespassing into strangers' homes, stealing pets, asking strangers if they wanted to die, and jumping on the backs of people while they cross the street. He claimed that he committed crimes in the name of the "we outside movement," which focuses on not letting others restrict your actions. He has since been banned from TikTok and YouTube. 

Before Fame

He began sharing his content at the age of 8 after being kicked out of school three times.  

Trivia

He was arrested in 2023 after entering the home of a family, causing them to fear for their lives. His court-given restrictions were broken two days after he was released by the court. He justifies his actions largely due to his race, a diagnosis of ADHD, and being raised without a father figure.  

Family Life

He was raised in a single-parent home. 

Associated With

He and Piers Morgan reached the point of confrontation when he was interviewed on Morgan's show.
