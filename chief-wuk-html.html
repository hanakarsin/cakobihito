Aug 17, 2024 Hettie Henneman

Chief Wuk - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper known for tracks like "Turkey Season," "DOA" and "Ain't Hiding." His song "I Ain't lying" features EST Gee. He is the co-founder of the music label OTF, which stands for Only the Family. 

Before Fame

He grew up with Lil Durk and Fredo Santana.

Trivia

In 2017, he dropped "Million Dollar Dreams Federal Nightmares."  He has earned more than 160,000 followers on his wukaduk Instagram account where he posts updates about his career, album art, photos from performances and more.

Family Life

He is from Chicago. He was raised by his mother and grandmother.

Associated With

His song "Thug Life" features Icewear Vezzo.
