Federal Government Reduces Fuel Price To N121.50

May 25, 2024 In order to reduce the stress of COVID-19 pandemic on the lively hood of many Nigerians, the Federal government had announced a new fuel price band of N121.50  per litre for Premium Motor Spirit, also known as petrol.
