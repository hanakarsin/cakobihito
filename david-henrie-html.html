David Henrie - Age, Family, Bio

Known for his roles as Justin Russo in Wizards of Waverly Place, Ted Mosby's son on How I Met Your Mother, and Larry on That's So Raven.

Before Fame

He began riding dirt bikes, skateboarding, and snowboarding at a young age and landed a role in The Pitts as Petey Pitt in 2004.

Trivia

In 2011, he was nominated for a Kids' Choice Award for Favorite TV Sidekick for his role in Wizards of Waverly Place.

Family Life

He was born to talent manager Linda Henrie and producer James Henrie. He has a brother, actor Lorenzo Henrie. He married Maria Cahill in 2017. They welcomed a daughter named Pia in 2019. Their second child, a son named James, arrived on December 25, 2020. They announced in December 2021 that they are expecting their third child together. 

Associated With

He played a major role in Wizards of Waverly Place with Selena Gomez.
