'Obedience Is Better Than Sack Of Rice'

Blessing Douglas, the daughter-in-law of veteran actress, Joke Silva has reacted following the decision of the 61-year-old thespian to support the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu.

Naija News earlier reported that the actress joined the wife of the Lagos State Governor, Ibijoke Sanwolu and other women in Lagos on Monday for the APC rally in the State.

Joke Silva took to her Instagram page to share photos and videos of the APC women’s rally at Onikan Stadium.

Bearing in mind the series of attacks online from many youths and fans, the actress turned off her comment section as she may not want to respond to attacks from netizens.

Notwithstanding many Nigerians took to Twitter to drag her, faulting her decision to support the former Governor of Lagos.

I Cover Myself, Child With Blood Of Jesus

Amidst the social media attack, her daughter-in-law, Blessing Douglas, took to her Instagram page to share cryptic posts and this left us wondering if she is still married to the son of the actress.

Blessing made a mockery of the situation stating that she and her child are exempted from all the curses and insults that netizens are hurling at Joke Silva and her family.

She further urged Nigerians to respect other people’s choices and vote their conscience with the hope that the country will get better.

In her words: “Just like that, stray bullet just day touch person watowato, obedience is better than sack of rice. I cover myself and my child with the blood of Jesus o. We no do uno anything beg.”

“3rd generation ati 4th generation fire, Twitter is wild mehn. Respect peoples choices! Get your PVC! Vote your conscience! Hopefully, things will get better. Until the, may Nigeria not happen to you.”
