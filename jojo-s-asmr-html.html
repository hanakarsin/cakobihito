Jojo's ASMR - Age, Family, Bio

ASMR content creator on YouTube whose relaxing and tingle-inducing videos are meant to help his 2.2 million subscribers fall asleep more quickly. He is often seen doing card tricks in his videos.

Before Fame

He launched his channel in January of 2015, but did not upload a first video until Christmas day 2016. The video was called "The Best ASMR Video Ever | Instant Tingles!"

Trivia

He is a piano player, as seen in several videos on his ayyjonah Instagram account.

Family Life

His real name is Jonah. He has a sister named Sasha.

Associated With

Some of his ASMR inspirations include Heather Feather and RaphyTaffyASMR.
