'I Wasn't There To Kiss Her'

Ahead of the premiere of the Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Reunion, housemates have continued to churn out content to make fans anticipate the show.

Barely a few days after Maria revealed how Beatrice did her dirty during the reunion in leaked audio, the latter has bragged about giving her a sounding slap.

Responding to critics who have always body-shamed her for having bad breath, Beatrice in a post shared on Twitter said she wasn’t at the reunion to kiss Maria but was there to give her a slap.

Beatrice said it is understandable if Maria claims to have perceived the breath of a thunderous slap which will make her not repeat the word she had said to her.

She wrote: “I wasn’t there to kiss her. I was there to give her a sounding slap… so if she claimed she perceived the breath of a thunderous slap that made her not to reaped the word…it’s understandable”
