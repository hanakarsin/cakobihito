Yemi Solade Reveals The Cause of Osmond Gbadebo's Death As Nollywood Stars Mourn

Veteran Nollywood actor Yemi Solade has revealed the cause of the sudden death of his colleague Gbadebo Osmond.

The death of Gbadebo Osmond hit the Yoruba Nollywood industry late Monday night, 30th May 2022.

With lots of people still wondering about the cause of the death as the actor seemed hale and hearty, Yemi Solade while reacting to the post on an Instagram blog revealed that Osmond has been battling a terminal ailment for years and he lost the fight.

Yemi Solade wrote: “He battled a terminal ailment for years. Lost the fight. Adieu OSMOND”

Meanwhile, his colleagues Madam Saje, Adeniyi Johnson, Bimbo Sucess Saidi Balogun, Tawa Ajesefinni, Ronke Oshodi-Oke, and others took to their social media pages to mourn him.

adenyijohnson wrote: “Osmoood!!!! This hits differently that I can’t even post your picture … Every soul around us knows I love you… how I wish ooooooo ahhhhhhh”

kiitanbukola wrote: “So so painful , this one hit me hard Walahi”

sajetiologa wrote: “Good night osmond”

bimbosuccess wrote: “So sad osmond gbadebo ,May your gentle soul continue to Rest In perfect Peace!!! ile aiye,ile aisan vanity upon vanity”

saidibalogun wrote: “RIP Osmond Gbadebo”

ronkeoshodioke wrote: “It’s very……….. Had for me to say good night Gbadebo Oṣùmo but God knows the best. Rip my darling friend”

Gbadebo Osmond was also a movie producer who has featured in over 100 Nollywood movies.
