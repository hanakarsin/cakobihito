Shettima's Outfit At NBA Conference Spur New Challenge On Twitter (Photos)

Nigerians on the micro blogging platform, Twitter are mocking the outfit of the vice presidential candidate of the All Progressive Congress (APC), Kashim Shettima, at the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) Annual conference held in Lagos.

Naija News reports that the NBA annual conference was held yesterday at the Eko Hotels and Suites, Victoria Island, Lagos.

Shettima, like other top politicians and company heads, was present at the event to represent the presidential candidate of the APC, Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

However, the dress style of the former governor of Borno state was rather unusual and has stirred reactions, including from a former presidential aide, Reno Omokri, who slammed him for wearing a pair of gym shoes to the event while on a suit.

Other Nigerians, mostly young men, have created a trend called #ShettimaChallenge with images of several of them donning various sizes of suit with a red tie, and pairs of gym shoes across Nigerian twitter to mock the APC chieftain over his outfit
