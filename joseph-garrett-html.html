Stampy - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gaming phenomenon specializing in Minecraft who is known as Stampy, Stampylongnose, Stampylonghead, or Mr. Stampy Cat. His Stampylonghead channel has earned over 10 million subscribers. His real name is Joseph Garrett. 

Before Fame

He created his first channel Stampylongnose when Let's Plays were first becoming popular and left his job at a pub to create videos full time.

Trivia

He was hired by GamesThirst after posting a review about a game called Homefront for them. In 2014, he and Maker Studios announced a partnership to launch an educational YouTube channel. 

Family Life

He is originally from Portsmouth. He has done sister challenge videos with his sister, Netty Plays. He married YouTuber Kye Garrett aka Sqaishey on July 28, 2019.

Associated With

He collaborated with fellow YouTuber Shay Carl Butler on the web series Wonder Quest.
