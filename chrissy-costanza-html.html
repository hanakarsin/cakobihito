Chrissy Costanza - Age, Family, Bio

Lead vocalist for the New York based pop rock band Against the Current who released the EP titled Infinity in 2014. They first became known for their cover songs of popular artists on YouTube. 

Before Fame

She joined Against the Current in 2011 when she met bandmates Daniel Gow and Will Ferri through a mutual friend. 

Trivia

She is also a beauty guru and vlogging sensation on her own self-titled YouTube channel. 

Family Life

She has a younger brother named Mikey. She has dated guitarist Cameron Hurley from We Are the In Crowd. She subsequently began dating Brandon Martinez. She then started dating Tucker Roberts, who is president of Spectacor Gaming and son of Comcast CEO Brian L. Roberts. They currently reside in LA together with their 3 cats.

Associated With

She and Against the Current did a collaboration with Alex Goot on the cover of "Good Time" by Owl City and Carly Rae Jepsen that really helped grow their name.
