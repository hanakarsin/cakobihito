PDP Presidential Candidate, Atiku Returns From Vacation (Video)

The Presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Atiku Abubakar has returned to Nigeria after his vacation in Dubai.

The former Vice president had jetted out of the country shortly after the presidential primary of the main opposition party.

Naija News learnt that Atiku after his trip to Dubai crossed to the United Kingdom (UK).

Atiku was welcomed by his supporters at the airport, who chanted “Atiku is coming” and “Welcome Daddy.”

The PDP presidential candidate is expected to attend the governorship rally of the party in Osun State today to rally support for the governorship candidate of the party, Ademola Adeleke.

Atiku would be in the company of the party’s national chairman, Iyorchia Ayu, governors elected on the platform of the party and some party leaders.

Naija News had earlier reported that Atiku’s running mate and Governor of Delta State, Ifeanyi Okowa, Edo State Governor, Godwin Obaseki, Sokoto State Governor, Aminu Tambuwal and Bayelsa State Governor, Duoye Diri have arrived in the state earlier for the rally which will be held at the Osogbo Township Stadium.

Atiku and the PDP would be hoping to win the governorship election in Osun State which will be held on the 16th of July.
