Despite 'Beef' With 2face, Davido Showers Love On His First Daughter Ehi

Despite their rumoured beef, Nigerian Afrobeat singer Davido has showered love on Ehi, the daughter of legendary singer 2face Idiabia.

It is no longer news that Davido has for a long time been on a cold beef with 2face Idibia for consistently failing to acknowledge his achievements in the music industry.

It became evident in February 2022 after Davido held his concert at the O2 Arena in London, and many fans and some celebrities congratulated him except 2face Idiba, Burna Boy, Tiwa Savage and many others.

Angered by how he was ignored by 2face, Davido unfollowed the legendary singer on the video and photo-sharing app, Instagram.

However, a video shared by 2face baby mama Pero Adeniyi on his Instagram page captured Davido giving a birthday shout-out to 2face’s daughter.

He said: A big Happy birthday to Ehi, hope you had a great day, one love, more life enjoy your day, as you get older may God continue to bless you.

Meanwhile, Davido’s shout to 2face’s daughter is barely 24 hours after the legendary singer was attacked on his Instagram page to celebrate Burna Boy for his performance at the ‘One Night In Space’ concert but ignored Davido during his time.
