Jinder Mahal - Age, Family, Bio

Indian-American who became a rising star of the WWE with a feud with Ted DiBiase and a conspiracy to take down Kane.

Before Fame

After a stint in the WWE farm league, Florida Championship Wrestling, he developed the Indian wrestler persona, turban and all.

Trivia

In a classic WWE moment, he instigated a six superstar ambush of Kane that was thwarted when Kane's brother, The Undertaker, showed up.

Family Life

He grew up in Calgary, Alberta, trained by his uncle Gama Singh.

Associated With

He nabbed the WWE Championship title from wrestler Randy Orton in 2017.
