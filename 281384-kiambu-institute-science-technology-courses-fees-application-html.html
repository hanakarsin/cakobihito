Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) courses, fees &amp; application

Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) is among the top-ranking higher learning institutions in the country. It offers world-class academic programs and is an excellent centre for learners interested in pursuing careers in entrepreneurship, industrial practices, and other vocational studies.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology offers affordable academic programs attracting several learners nationwide. It offers its courses under the oversight of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Technology. The school sits about fourteen kilometres outside Nairobi City and was established solely on the Harambee effort in 1973.

Courses offered at Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology

The Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology offers various programs in science, technology, engineering, and related fields. KIST aims to provide quality education and training to its students to prepare them for careers in the dynamic fields of science and technology. Below are the KIST courses and requirements.

What are the requirements for KIST application?

The mandatory requirements you need to have to join the institution are as follows:

  • Fee payment slip/cheque.
  • Admission letter.
  • KCSE result slip or certificate.
  • KCPE result slip or certificate
  • Form four school leaving certificate.
  • National identity card.
  • Birth certificate.
  • Digital passport (40mm by 50mm) photograph.
  • Duly signed medical form.
  • Duly signed student guide declaration form.
  • Essential tools and books.

Note: You need to have an original and copy of the above certificates.

KIST school fees payment

KIST fee structure for certificate courses and diploma courses is designed to be affordable for all students. Each course requires a payment of Ksh. 28,210 per term, except for artisan in baking programs. For the first term, they pay Ksh. 40,050, and for the second and third terms, they pay Ksh. 35,050 each. Where is the Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology located?

The institution is located in Kiambu County, off Thika-Kangari road. It's 14 kilometres from the City of Nairobi.

What grade should you have to join KIST?

The minimum grade required to join KIST is a mean grade of C- (minus) or above for diploma programs and a minimum mean grade of D+ (plus) or D (plain) for certificate programs.

Does Kiambu Institute of Science and Technology offer hostels?

KIST provides hostel accommodation for students, but it's important to note that this accommodation is a privilege, not a right. It can be revoked if students fail to adhere to KIST policies, rules, and regulations.
