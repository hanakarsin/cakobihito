BREAKING: NFF Glass House On Fire

Latest reports coming in state that the  headquarters of the Nigeria Football Federation (NFF) in the Wuse Zone 7 District of Abuja is currently on fire.

The origin of the fire is still unclear at the moment, but NFF spokesperson, Ademola Olajire, has confirmed the incident.

Mr. Olajire told journalists he was just coming to the office and could confirm that the fire began on the third floor of the building.

Premium Times reporter near the area said that offices of the President, Secretary General and the accounts department might have been evacuated.

As of now, fire service officials are now trying hard to end the fire and stop it from spreading.
