Security Operatives Kill Three Gunmen In Anambra State

Security operatives have neutralized three gunmen during a gun battle in Anambra State.

Naija News gathered that security operatives including the police and Military on Wednesday, killed the three fierce-looking gunmen in combat.

The was reported to have happened at Umunze, Orumba South local government area.

The gunmen were also reported to have different charms on their bodies, including legs, necks, waists and hands.

Naija News understands that the gunmen were part of the group killing innocent citizens around the Orumba area.

All efforts to reach the Anambra Police spokesman Ikenga Tochukwu proved abortive, but one of the senior police officers in the Command confirmed the incident to the Nation newspaper.

He said a combined team of the Police and Military carried out the operation around 4:30 pm this evening.
