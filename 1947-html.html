1947 Chinese Zodiac | Fire Pig

With the influence of the Fire Element the Fire Pig is amongst the most intense members of the Pig sign. The confidence of the Fire Pig is hard to match and members of the sign are known for being bold and courageous in their actions. Unlike other calculated members of their sign, the Fire Pig has an eye for adventure, which explains why it is not unusual for the Fire Pig to act on a whim. In their social pursuits, the Fire Pig is idealistic and generous. Although they have firm material ambitions, the Fire Pig may be most content with a strong, close knit family and friend group.
