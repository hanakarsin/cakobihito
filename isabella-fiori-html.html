Bella Fiori - Age, Family, Bio

Specializing in beauty and travel content, this Australian YouTuber and Instagram sensation has racked up more than 2.4 million subscribers on her Bella Fiori YouTube channel and more than 550,000 followers on Instagram. She has had paid partnerships with beauty companies like Mecca and Dermalogica. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel in March of 2015 but began posting on Instagram as early as November of 2013. 

Trivia

She became a brand ambassador for the fashion line Sisters. She has posted true crime videos and conspiracy theory content on YouTube. 

Family Life

Her first name is Isabella. She is from Brisbane, Australia. She started dating Kerry Jull in June of 2013. 

Associated With

She has created YouTube videos about Madeleine McCann, JonBenet Ramsey, and Princess Diana, all of which have accumulated over 1 million views.
