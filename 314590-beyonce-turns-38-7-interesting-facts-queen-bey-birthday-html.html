Beyonc turns 38: 7 Interesting facts about Queen Bey on her birthday

- Beyoncé Giselle Knowles, aka Queen Bey, celebrated her birthday on Wednesday, September 4

- She has risen through the ranks of music to become one of the most celebrated and influential female artistes in the world

- In celebration of the renowned singer and actress, TUKO.co.ke compiled a list of seven interesting facts about her life

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles needs no introduction, but for those only stumbling out from under a rock, Queen Bey, as she is also known, is a US multi-platinum, Grammy award-winning recording artist who is acclaimed for her thrilling vocals, videos and live shows.

TUKO.co.ke put together some interesting facts about Beyoncé in celebration of her 38th birthday.

Beyonce was born on September 4, 1981 in Houston, Texas.

Here are some interesting facts abut her:

1. Beyoncé first captured the public’s eye as the lead vocalist of R&B group, Destiny’s Child, alongside her cousin, Kelly Rowland and two classmates.

2. She later established a solo career with her debut album, Dangerously In Love, becoming one of the music’s top-selling artists with sold-out tours and a string of five Grammy awards.

In 2013, she released her fifth studio album, self-titled Beyoncé and performed twice at the Super Bowl.

She released her sixth studio album, Lemonade in 2016, and two years later dropped a joint album with Jay-Z called Everything Is Love while both were on tour together.

3. Beyoncé also holds an acting career and has starred in several films including Dream Girls, Pink Panther, Austin Powers, and of late, The Lion King.

4. She married hip-hop recording artist, Jay-Z, in a small, private ceremony in New York City in 2008.

Among the guests were Beyoncé's mother, Tina Knowles, her father and manager, Matthew Knowles, her sister, Solange, Destiny Child members, Kelly Rowland and Michelle Williams, and her friend, Gwyneth Paltrow.

5. In 2017, Queen Bey was named the highest-earning female musician of the year. According to Forbes, Beyoncé snagged approximately KSh 15.3 billion in pretax income in June 2016 through to June 2017.

She outpaced the earnings of fellow divas, Adele's KSh 7.08 billion and Taylor Swift (KSh 4.5 billion).

Forbes attributed the windfall to the singer’s success of her album Lemonade and her Formation World Tour.

6. Beyoncé and Jay-Z welcomed their first child, Blue Ivy Carter on January 7, 2012.

The couple then welcomed their twins, a boy and a girl in June 2017 and later filed trademark documents at the United States Patent and Trademark Office for the names Sir and Rumi.

7. The latest Beyoncé news to hit media was this July, when the star featured as Nala in the premiere of The Lion King.

She voiced the childhood friend-turned-love-interest of Simba, voiced by Danny Glover.

Beyoncé also curated and produced a companion album for the production, titled The Lion King: The Gift, and released the single, Spirit around the time of the film’s release.
