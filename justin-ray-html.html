Justin Ray - Age, Family, Bio

Independent film actor as well as dancer who has starred in movies called 1 Chance 2 Dance, Apocalypse LA and Attack of the Killer Donuts, among several others. He was also featured in the web series Living With Models.

Before Fame

He obtained an AA degree at a community college before transferring to UC Irvine, where he majored in drama.

Trivia

He has performed on stage in productions of Wicked, West Side Story, CATS, and a couple of Shakespeare plays. He was a dancer on Glee for an episode.

Family Life

He has a sister named Katelyn and an older brother as well.

Associated With

He starred alongside Katie Savoy in Living with Models.
