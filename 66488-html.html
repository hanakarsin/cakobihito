EXCLUSIVE: Kim And Kanye's Wedding To Take Place In Fortress With Tragic History

Finally, some details of the most expected wedding of the year has become known.

It was learnt that Kim Kardashian and Kanye West’s wedding will take place at a 16th century castle in Florence, Italy. According to the couple's close friend, the event is scheduled for next weekend with 1000 guests invited.

A place where the fairytale wedding is going to happen is a Florentine fortification Forte di Belveder on the banks of the river Arno. The venue was chosen not without purpose, the rapper admitted that their beloved daughter North West was conceived there.

Kanye believes that Florence is the most beautiful European city and one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Moreover, the future spouses celebrated Kim’s 32nd birthday in 2012 ibidem. This birthday celebration was perceived by the couple as their first honeymoon.

Despite Kim has not been seen in Italy lately Kanye has spent several days here dealing with the final plans for the wedding. The previous plans to wed in France were changed to a pre-wedding dinner in Paris on May 23 with the next-day actual wedding ceremony in Florence.

Forte di Belveder has a tragic history as two young people fell from the ramparts in 2006 and 2008. After the late incident the fortress was shut for an investigation and was reopened last year. Now it is accessible for everyone who wants to enjoy the breathtaking views of the Renaissance masterpieces.
