Guardiola Sends Crucial Message to Chelsea Boss Ahead of Their Premier League Meeting at Etihad Stad

- Pep Guardiola faces Thomas Tuchel in the Premier League this weekend

- The game will serve as a dress rehearsal for their Champions League final this month

- The Blues will need to win the game to consolidate their fourth position on the table

- Guardiola has now showered encomiums on the German boss for his impact on his team

Manchester City boss Pep Guardiola has commended Chelsea under the tutelage of Thomas Tuchel after reaching the Champions League final this season.

The Blues dispatched Real Madrid 3-1 on aggregate after their semifinal fixtures to set up an all-English final in Istanbul later this month.

Chelsea became formidable again after hiring the German boss as a replacement for Frank Lampard in January.

The former Borussia Dortmund handler has also guided the London club to the final of the FA Cup where they face Leicester City and Pep says he has nothing but respect for Tuchel. Guardiola was quoted by Metro UK saying:

Their careers might be miles apart in terms of silverware, but there is no question both Thomas Tuchel and Pep Guardiola are two of the best managers in world football at the moment.

Before being appointed Chelsea boss in January 2020, Tuchel previously faced Guardiola five times (twice with Mainz and three times with Borussia Dortmund) and has failed to beat the Spaniard in all of them.

However, he got his first victory over Guardiola in the FA Cup semi-final where Chelsea eliminated Manchester City to set up a final against Leicester City.

Fate seems to love this rivalry as the pair will clash again twice before the end of the season, with the finale set to be on the biggest stage in European football - the Champions League final.

Chelsea players have got all the motivation they needed to win their second Champions League title when they face fellow Premier League club Manchester City in Istanbul later this month.
