In a separate story, a woman narrated the sad tale of a candidate who blew his chance to get a job in the first five minutes of walking into the office after he dismissively interacted with the receptionist, the Mirror reported.

According to the lady, the receptionist said hello to the man but he seemed uninterested in engaging in a friendly conversation with her.

"He made no eye contact and clearly showed no interest in speaking with her," the woman narrated.
