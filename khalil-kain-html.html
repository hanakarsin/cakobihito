Khalil Kain - Age, Family, Bio

Known best for playing Raheem Porter in the early 1990s crime movie Juice, this New York-born actor also portrayed Darnell Wilkes in the early 2000s situation comedy Girlfriends.

Before Fame

Early in his career, he appeared with Gregory Hines and Danny DeVito in the 1994 movie Renaissance Man.

Trivia

He portrayed golfer Tiger Woods in a Showtime movie titled The Tiger Woods Story.

Family Life

A native of New York, New York, he grew up in a family of Chinese and African American heritage. He later fathered two children: Noa and Luka.

Associated With

He replaced fellow actor and New York native Flex Alexander in the role of Darnell on Season 2 of the UPN and CW series Girlfriends.
