Skye Sweetnam - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian singer who released such singles as "Billy S." (in 2003) and "Tangled Up in Me" (in 2004). She was nominated for a Juno Award in 2006 in the New Artist of the Year category. She later adopted the stage name Sever and became the lead singer of the band Sumo Cyco. 

Before Fame

She started singing and dancing for family and friends when she was five years old.

Trivia

She provided the singing voice of Barbie in the 2006 film The Barbie Diaries.

Family Life

She was born to Deirdre and Greg Sweetnam and was raised with a brother and a sister.

Associated With

She was the opening act for Britney Spears on Spears' 2004 Onyx Hotel Tour.
