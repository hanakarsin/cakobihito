Ladan Bosso Invites 40 Players For WAFU B U20 As Camp Opens

Head Coach of Nigeria’s U20 Boys (Flying Eagles), Ladan Bosso has called up a total of 40 players for next month’s WAFU B U20 Championship taking place in Niamey, capital of Niger Republic.

The list has 4 goalkeepers, 12 midfielders, 17 midfielders and seven forwards.

Invited players have been advised to arrive in Abuja on Monday, 18th April with their international passports, according to Team Secretary.

Seven–time African champions, the Flying Eagles will play in Group B of the WAFU B Championship alongside Ghana and Burkina Faso, with the tournament scheduled for 7th – 20th May in Niamey.

Hosts Niger Republic, Cote d’Ivoire, Togo and Benin Republic will contend in Group A.

Only the two finalists will qualify from the WAFU B zone to participate in the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations.
