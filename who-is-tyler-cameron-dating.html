Tyler Cameron Is Single and Hints at Possible Return as Bachelor

Tyler Cameron has made it clear that he's single and ready to mingle after several public relationships. But who is the model dating now?

Former Bachelorette contestant Tyler Cameron has become everyone's favorite second choice. After being sent home heartbroken in 2019 the finale of Hannah Brown's season, everyone instantly fell in love with Tyler.

Since the season aired, Tyler has been busy flipping houses and dating some of Hollywood's hottest single ladies. So, what is the reality star's relationship status today? Keep reading for Tyler's dating history, and if he is still single.

Tyler Cameron first met Hannah Brown on 'The Bachelorette.'

We all watched as Tyler competed on The Bachelorette for Hannah's heart, and we all were devastated when she chose cheating, lying Jed Wyatt instead of Tyler. After she found out that Jed had a girlfriend back home before coming on the show, Hannah then had the guts to ask Tyler out on a date again.

There were rumors about their relationship, but both of them have confirmed separately that they are just friends.

"I always say, 'If I had the chance to date Gigi Hadid, I'd probably do it too,'" Hannah said of Tyler moving on with Gigi Hadid. "It was all just really hard from my side of things, having to navigate being in the public eye and really not knowing what happened was hard."

Yes, Tyler Cameron then dated Gigi Hadid in 2019.

While Gigi Hadid was in an in-between phase with Zayn Malik, Tyler slid into Gigi's Instagram DMs and the pair were an item for all of two months. But it was a heavy two months, with Tyler attending Gigi's grandmother's funeral with her and even looking for apartments in Gigi's neighborhood in New York City.

If you asked Tyler about it, though, he would adamantly deny that they were a thing, claiming they were just "friends." Yeah, OK, buddy.

Then Tyler Cameron was with Stassie Karanikolaou.

Tyler wastes no time while he's single — and honestly, we don't blame him. It wasn't long after he and Gigi were confirmed to be over that Tyler was spotted kissing a mysterious blonde. Surprise, surprise, it was Kylie Jenner's BFF, Stassie Karanikolaou.

Like Tyler's done with the rest of his recent flings, he hasn't confirmed if he and Stassie are (or were) really dating, so it's probably safe to say that things aren't too serious between them. But they were both at David Dobrik's party together.

Tyler Cameron has dated several models including Juliette Porter and Camila Kendra.

Just before the 2021 Super Bowl, the model was seen with Siesta Key star Juliette Porter, according to US Weekly.

“They seemed like they were having fun,” a source told the outlet. Tyler was also with his fellow Bachelor Nation bros Peter Weber, Jason Tartick, and Blake Horstmann.

Tyler also dated models Camila Kendra in 2021, and Paige Lorenze in 2022.

“We just had to take a step back,” Tyler told E! News of his split from Paige. “It wasn’t the right timing … We’re back [to], you know, not dating anymore, just doing our own thing right now. We both have a bunch of respect and love for each other but just not the right time for both of us right now.”

In 2022, Tyler was linked to Kristin Cavallari — but it was short-lived.

Tyler was rumored to be dating former The Hills star Kristin Cavallari in late 2022. The duo released a steamy campaign for Kristin's jewelry line, Uncommon James, and were spotted getting cozy at a New Year's Eve party per Page Six.

"Tyler I've known for about a year," Kristin said on the Broad Ideas With Rachel Bilson podcast. "He is one of the nicest, most down-to-earth, laid-back on the planet. He was my New Year's date. We talk a lot. We're not dating. I think he's such a great guy [but] we're both so busy."

Tyler is currently single, but is being the next Bachelor in his future?

Tyler is one of the stars of Special Forces Season 2, and single — but he is not against returning to reality TV to find love.

"I don’t believe in doing anything unless your heart is fully into it. And so I just don’t know,” he told the New York Post of being the next Bachelor. "I would never say no, though. Never say never. So I think, you know, it could happen later on in life."

We would definitely watch that!
