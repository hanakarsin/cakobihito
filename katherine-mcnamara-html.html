Katherine McNamara - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who starred in the 2013 comedy film Contest and Sonya in The Maze Runner films. In 2016, she began starring in the television series Shadowhunters and later had a recurring role as Mia in the CW's Arrow. In 2022, she began starring as Abbey in CW's Walker: Independence. She also landed supporting roles in New Year's Eve, Matchmaker Mary, and Girl Vs. Monster. 

Before Fame

She started her acting career in a Broadway production of A Little Nigh Music when she was just 13 years old. 

Trivia

She played Tiffany in the 2016 film Little Savages. In 2018, she won a People's Choice Award for Favorite Female TV Star for her role in Shadowhunters. For the same show, she won a Teen Choice Award in 2019 in the category of Choice TV Actress: Fantasy/Sci-Fi. 

Family Life

She was born to parents Evan and Ursula in Kansas City, Missouri. She moved to Los Angeles in 2011. 

Associated With

She once had a recurring role opposite Olivia Holt on Disney Channel's Kickin' It in 2012.
