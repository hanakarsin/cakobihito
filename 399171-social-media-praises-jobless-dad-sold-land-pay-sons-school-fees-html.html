Social media users praises jobless dad who sold land to pay son's school fees

- The man with the Twitter handle @sarnysane shared screenshot of the conversation he had with his father over the land

- In the conversation, the father said he sold the land because of the love he has for his son,

- He also added that he knows the young man would build a big house for him in the future
