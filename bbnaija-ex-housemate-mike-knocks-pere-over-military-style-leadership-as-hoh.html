BBNaija Ex-Housemate, Mike Knocks Pere Over 'Military' Style Leadership As HOH

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Mike Edwards has slammed ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Head of House (HOH), Pere, following his address last night.

Naija News reports that Pere emerged as the Big Brother Naija Shine Ya Eye Head of House after he victoriously got the highest points earning the most coveted office for the third week.

However, to stamp his authority, Pere, last night, addressed the housemates, demanding that they accord due respect to the position he is occupying.

The speech caused a stir,  with a lot of reactions from social media, as the viewers accused him of adopting ‘MILITARY REGIME’ RULE style.

Amongst, displeased viewers, is former housemate, Mike, who questioned Pere’s behaviour whilst berating him for allowing the HOH to get into his head.

Reacting to the video, Mike, via the microblogging platform, Twitter, wrote; ‘HOH can drive some people craze..wetin be this kind authority, abeg oga shift’.
