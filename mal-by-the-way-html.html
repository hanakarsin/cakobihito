Mal By the Way - Age, Family, Bio

Podcaster and Hip-Hop influencer best known for his work co-hosting The Joe Budden Podcast with founder Joe Budden and fellow co-host Rory. The three men are childhood friends, though Mal did not join the podcast until episode 77 and then wasn't a permanent fixture until he and his stories became popular with fans. 

Before Fame

He's been based in New York City. 

Trivia

One of the stories that captured fans attentions describe a period of days during which he and Budden spent time in jail together. 

Family Life

He's the brother of Roc-a-fella Record's Kareem "Biggs" Burke and Kyambo "Hip Hop" Joshua.

Associated With

Because of his affiliation with Roc-a-fella, he's also been acquainted with Jay-Z.
