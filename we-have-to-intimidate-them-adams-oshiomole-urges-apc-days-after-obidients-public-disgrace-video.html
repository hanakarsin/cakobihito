'We Have To Intimidate Them'

The former governor of Edo State, Adams Oshiomole, has urged members of the All Progressives Congress (APC) to intimidate other political parties.

Naija News reports that this is coming a few days after the APC chieftain was publicly disgraced and chased out of Ring Road, in Benin City by ‘Obidients’, supporters of the Labour Party (LP) presidential candidate, Peter Obi.

In a video sighted online, a popular APC member in Edo State, Tony Kabaka, and other members of APC were seen having a meeting with Adams Oshiomole.

According to Oshiomole, he must live up to expectations as the APC leader in Edo and will not allow other political parties to take over.

He further said other political parties are minority boys and APC members should intimidate them.

However, there are speculations online that the instruction is targeted at supporters of LP.

Adam Oshiomhole Humiliated At NLC Conference

In related news, a video in which Adams Oshiomhole was booed at the 13th National Labour Congress Conference has surfaced on social media.

Adam Oshiomhole and the presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC) Bola Tinubu were invited to the 13th National Delegates Conference of the NLC which was held at the International Conference Center, Abuja on Tuesday.

The conference tagged “Building People’s Power, National Unity and Quest for a New Social Contract” had Vice President Yemi Osinbajo as a Special Guest of Honor.

However, following the failure of the APC flag bearer to show up for the event, the former Edo governor took to the podium to speak on his behalf but was met with an agitated crowd who started to boo him.
