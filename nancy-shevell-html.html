Nancy Shevell - Age, Family, Bio

Businesswoman who is best known as the third wife of rock and roll icon and former Beatles member Paul McCartney. She and Paul got married on October 9, 2011.

Before Fame

She first met Paul at the Hamptons in New York in the early 1990s.

Trivia

Paul's song "My Valentine," from the 2012 album Kisses on the Bottom, is about Nancy.

Family Life

She has a son named Arlen from her marriage to New York attorney Bruce Blakeman. She is a second cousin of Barbara Walters.

Associated With

Her husband played alongside John Lennon in The Beatles.
