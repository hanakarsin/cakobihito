Marc Storace - Age, Family, Bio

Maltese singer-songwriter who has been associated with the Swiss heavy metal band Krokus.

Before Fame

He began singing with two local bands, Stonehenge Union and The Boys, when he was only fourteen years old.

Trivia

He performed the role of a native medicine man in the 2006 German film Anuk.

Family Life

He was one of six children born to Anthony Storace and Edna Crockford.

Associated With

His three-octave voice has been compared to that of Led Zeppelin vocalist, Robert Plant.
