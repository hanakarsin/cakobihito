Josue Vargas - Age, Family, Bio

Professional boxer who is known for having earned representation under the TMT boxing club. Going under the name The Prodigy, he shares clips of his fighting along with other promotional content through his Instagram for his over 110,000 followers. 

Before Fame

He was first signed to the Floyd Mayweather Jr. brand Mayweather Promotions at the age of 17. He went on to share fighting and boxing related content to Instagram in August of 2015. 

Trivia

He shared a photo with Cardi B when she performed at the T-Mobile Arena for weigh-ins in August of 2017. 

Family Life

He was born in Aguadilla, Puerto Rico and was later raised in the Bronx in New York. 

Associated With

Floyd Mayweather Jr. posted a video of them sparring together without asking permission and behind closed doors.
