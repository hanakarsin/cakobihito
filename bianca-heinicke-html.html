Bianca Heinicke - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion, beauty, and lifestyle video blogger who would become the most successful German female YouTuber in 2014. Her channel has more than 5 million subscribers. She also runs the musical.ly account Bibi & Julian with her partner Julienco. 

Before Fame

She published her first video in December of 2012 and began her channel with a series of hair braiding tutorials. 

Trivia

She is a social media phenomenon on Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. She has earned over 8 million on Instagram alone. Her Instagram has featured numerous bikini photos and photos of her family. 

Family Life

She is from Cologne, Germany. She and Julienco became engaged in July of 2018; they married that September and welcomed their first child a month later. Their second child, a daughter named Emily, arrived on March 20, 2020. They later divorced in the spring of 2022. She began dating Timothy Hill the same year. 

Associated With

She has collaborated with fellow YouTubers Dagi Bee and Lionttv.
