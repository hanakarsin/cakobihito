Asher Blinkoff - Age, Family, Bio

Child voice actor first known for his role in the film Hotel Transylvania 2 in the role of Dennis. He also performed a voice role for the 2016 adaptation of The Jungle Book.

Before Fame

He was featured in an ESPN fantasy football commercial and a Huggies commercial as an infant. He previously did voice work for a couple of shorts called Taking Flight and Wake-up Call.

Trivia

He grew up loving to swim, as well as play baseball and soccer. He is also an avid Minecraft gamer.

Family Life

He is the son of Saul Blinkoff, a director and animator, and mother Marion, an Associate Producer for Disney and Nickelodeon.

Associated With

He and Selena Gomez were both cast in the film Hotel Transylvania 2.
