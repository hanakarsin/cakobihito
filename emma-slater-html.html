Emma Slater - Age, Family, Bio

English professional dancer and choreographer known for her appearances on the American version of Dancing with the Stars. She won the British Under 21 Latin American Championships at the Blackpool Dance Festival in 2005. 

Before Fame

She began training in Ballroom and Latin American dance when she was 10. 

Trivia

At the age of 16, she acted in the UK play "Simply Ballroom." 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Sutton Coldfield, England alongside her twin sister Kelly. In 2016, she got engaged to her Dancing with the Stars co-star Sasha Farber. They finally tied the knot in March of 2018.

Associated With

She won DWTS season 24 with NFL player Rashad Jennings.
