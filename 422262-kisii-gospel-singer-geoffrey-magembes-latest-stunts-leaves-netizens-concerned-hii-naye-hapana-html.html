Kisii Gospel Singer Geoffrey Magembe's Latest Stunts Leave Kenyans Concerned

  • Geoffrey Magembe has hit the headlines again but this time round he is worrying Kenyans with the latest antics he showed on his music video
  • The gospel singer was filmed washing his hair with brown dry soil, dancing in a dirty river and chasing people
  • Netizens have asked that he gets mental help and be more creative than copy what his fellow Kisii gospel artiste does

Controversial Kisii gospel singer Geoffrey Magembe's latest video stunts have left concerned Kenyans worried about his health and lifestyle.

Embarambamba wannabe

The artiste was a month ago featured on TUKO.co.ke after shocking people with his antics in music videos that were compared to Chris Embarambaba.

In the video clips, Magembe could be seen rolling in the mud, being dragged by a motorbike on the road, to jumping from high walls.
